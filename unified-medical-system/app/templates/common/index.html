About Unified Medical System

The Unified Medical System (UMS) is a comprehensive healthcare solution designed for India. It integrates various aspects of healthcare management, including data sharing, appointment booking, medical record access, and patient support.

Our system utilizes cutting-edge machine learning technology to detect potential outbreaks of viral diseases and pandemics at an early stage. By analyzing patient symptoms collected from various regions, UMS aims to improve public health outcomes across India.

Our Mission

  • Facilitate efficient healthcare delivery
  • Enable data-driven disease surveillance
  • Empower patients with easy access to their health information
  • Improve patient education through an AI-powered chatbot interface
  • Foster collaboration between healthcare providers, patients, and health officials
